About Me

My name is Clay Crawley. I guess if I have tried to be anything in this crazy life it would be described as: Rennaissance Man. I have a Master's Degree in History and am fluent in several languages including French and Russian.

I've lived in several places for varying periods of time including France, Germany, Russia, and Ukraine. I also practice martial arts and have done so for about 30 years now. My main disciplines are Tae Kwon Do and Aikido but I would certainly like to learn more.

I like to consider myself as driven and a perfectionist. I certainly hope that's the case. Although I am always afraid of leaving my comfort zone, I seem to have an inner urge to keep doing it. So I guess coding is the next big challenge for me.
